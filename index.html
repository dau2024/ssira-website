About SSIRA

A national centre for revenue administration excellence

The South Sudan Institute of Revenue Administration supports the development of skilled revenue officers, customs professionals, clearing agents, freight forwarders and public finance leaders.

Vision

To become a trusted centre of excellence for revenue administration training in South Sudan and the region.

Mission

To deliver practical, ethical and professional training that strengthens national revenue collection and service delivery.
